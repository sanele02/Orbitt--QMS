General Questions

What is Orbit Queue Management System?

Orbit is a modern, web-based queue management system designed for businesses of all sizes. It helps you manage customer queues efficiently with real-time displays, voice announcements, and easy-to-use dashboard controls.

Do I need to install any software?

No! Orbit is completely web-based. You can access it from any device with a web browser - computers, tablets, or smartphones. No downloads or installations required.

What types of businesses can use Orbit?

Orbit works for any business with customer queues: clinics, hospitals, banks, government offices, retail stores, restaurants, salons, and more. It's designed to be flexible for different industries.

Technical Questions

How do I set up the display screen?

Simply open the Queue Display page on any device connected to your TV or monitor. Use fullscreen mode (F11) for the best experience. The display updates automatically in real-time.

Do voice announcements work on all devices?

Voice announcements work on most modern browsers and devices. Make sure your device volume is turned up and browser permissions allow audio playback.

What happens if my internet goes down?

Orbit requires an internet connection to function. However, your queue data is safely stored in the cloud and will be available as soon as your connection is restored.
